# Problem 136: Continuation of Parallelogram Line Integral

## Problem Statement

This page contains continuation of calculations for the line integral around a parallelogram, showing the final simplification steps.

## Solution

### Final Calculation

From the previous page, the total line integral:

$$8 + \left(\frac{35}{2} - 3\sin(1)\right) - 30 + (-1.5 + 3\sin(1))$$

$$= 8 + 17.5 - 30 - 1.5 = -6$$

## Answer

$$\boxed{-6}$$
