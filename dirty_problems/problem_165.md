# Problem 165: Continuation of Divergence Calculation

## Problem Statement

This page contains continuation of calculations for $\nabla \cdot [\mathbf{r}\nabla(1/r^3)]$, showing the final simplification steps.

## Solution

### Step 1: Sum of Partial Derivatives

From the previous calculations:

$$\nabla \cdot [\mathbf{r}\nabla(1/r^3)] = -9r^{-4} + 12r^{-6}(x^2 + y^2 + z^2)$$

### Step 2: Substitute $r^2 = x^2 + y^2 + z^2$

$$= -9r^{-4} + 12r^{-6}r^2$$

$$= -9r^{-4} + 12r^{-4}$$

### Step 3: Final Simplification

$$= 3r^{-4}$$

## Answer

$$\boxed{\nabla \cdot [\mathbf{r}\nabla(1/r^3)] = 3r^{-4}}$$
